About

CS Dojo is a space for anyone struggling in a computer science class to find the help they need. Anyone may come simply to have a place to do their work, to ask specific questions about concepts from class that they do not understand, to have some guidance in completing homework assignments, or to receive help studying for a test.

Rules of the Dojo

The rules of the dojo are simple -- don't be stupid, and don't be a jerk. But If you want specifics, the following should serve as pretty good guidelines:

  • • Keep Computer Area Clean
  • • No Food or Drink
  • • No Unauthorized Printing
  • • No Games or Unauthorized Internet (Facebook, Youtube, Twitter, and etc.)
  • • Log Out And Take Your Belongings With You When You Leave
  • • No PDA (Public Display of Affection)
